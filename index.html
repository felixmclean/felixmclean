For the past 25 years, Felix McLean, New York Hong Kong Paris born Microsoft approved writer, comedian, producer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist and multi-media content brand manager living in Brooklyn / L.A, has traversed continents, experienced culture, and tasted adventure whilst writing about his life.

McLean spent his formative years growing up in Tokyo and at twelve became a member of Mensa. At fifteen he was screwed out of a company that hadn't been invented yet. He also loves technology!

After falling in love with the fame in high school, Felix decided to pursue a career is art but decided against higher education (He is non-conformist). The week after graduation, Felix moved to New York City with his cat, to pursue his dream. Not surprisingly this lead to instant success.

He is currently a lives in Brooklyn, New York with his husband, Kevin, and their twin son and daughter.
